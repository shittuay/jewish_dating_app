Safety & Privacy

Your safety and privacy are our top priorities. Learn about our security measures and how we protect our community.

Account Security

  • Secure Authentication

    We use industry-standard encryption and secure password hashing to protect your account credentials.

  • Profile Verification

    All profiles are manually reviewed to ensure authenticity and maintain community standards.

  • Privacy Controls

    You have full control over your profile visibility and who can contact you.

Privacy Protection

  • Personal Information

    We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

  • Secure Communication

    All messages are encrypted and stored securely. We monitor for inappropriate content.

  • Photo Protection

    Profile photos are protected against unauthorized downloads and screenshots.

Community Guidelines

  • Respectful Behavior

    We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, hate speech, or inappropriate behavior.

  • Reporting System

    Easy-to-use reporting tools to flag inappropriate content or behavior. Our team reviews all reports promptly.

  • Account Moderation

    Violations of our community guidelines may result in temporary or permanent account suspension.

Meeting Safely

  • Public Places

    Always meet in public places for your first few dates. Choose well-lit, populated locations.

  • Transportation

    Use your own transportation or a trusted ride service. Don't rely on your date for rides.

  • Stay Connected

    Let a friend or family member know where you're going and who you're meeting.

Need Help?

If you encounter any safety concerns or need assistance:

Emergency: Call 911 or your local emergency services
Support: Email safety@jewishdating.com
Report an Issue: Use the "Report" button on any profile or message

Remember: Your safety is our priority. If something doesn't feel right, trust your instincts and report it.
