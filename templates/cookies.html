What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us make your experience better by:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Understanding how you use our site
  • Making our services more efficient
  • Providing personalized content

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like:

  • User authentication
  • Security features
  • Basic site functionality

These cookies cannot be disabled.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and settings:

  • Language preferences
  • Theme settings
  • Notification preferences

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website:

  • Page visit statistics
  • User behavior patterns
  • Site performance metrics

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements:

  • Personalized ad content
  • Ad performance tracking
  • Social media integration

How to Control Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can:

  • Delete all cookies from your browser
  • Set your browser to prevent cookies being placed
  • Use our cookie consent tool to manage preferences

To learn more about cookies and how to manage them, visit www.aboutcookies.org.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Here's how to access cookie settings in popular browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies

Third-Party Cookies
